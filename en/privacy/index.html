Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 15, 2026 · Effective: July 15, 2026

1. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to the Zixia Browser Android application and its official website. We respect your privacy and design browsing, reading, and utility features to work locally on your device whenever practical. Please read this policy before using Zixia Browser.

2. Data processed on your device

To provide browser features, Zixia may store or process browsing history, bookmarks, tabs, shortcuts, download history, reading progress, website cookies and cache, display and reading preferences, user scripts, and ad-blocking rules on your device. We do not centrally collect this local data unless you actively use a networked feature or submit content to a third party.

3. Permissions and their purposes

  • Network access: used to open websites, download files, update rules, and access online services you choose to use.
  • Files and media: used to save downloads, open local text, and import or export relevant data. Exact permissions depend on your Android version.
  • Microphone or speech capabilities: used only when you actively choose voice input, read aloud, or a related feature.
  • Notifications and other system capabilities: used for download progress and supported system notices.

You can manage permissions in Android settings. Disabling a required permission may prevent the related feature from working.

4. Online features and third-party services

When you actively use search, translation, the AI assistant, read aloud, third-party sign-in, sharing, rule subscriptions, or sponsored links, relevant search terms, page content, prompts, URLs, or necessary technical information may be sent to the provider you select. Third parties process data under their own privacy policies. Do not submit passwords, identity documents, payment details, or other sensitive information to AI or online services.

5. Website access and basic logs

When you visit the official website or download the APK, GitHub Pages, network operators, or delivery infrastructure may process basic technical information such as IP address, access time, requested URL, and browser type to deliver content, maintain security, and diagnose errors. We do not sell this information or use it to build cross-site advertising profiles.

6. Sharing, transfers, and disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We do not share, transfer, or publicly disclose personal information to unrelated parties except to deliver a feature you actively choose, comply with applicable law, protect users or the public, or act with your explicit consent.

7. Retention and deletion

Local data normally remains on your device until you clear it in the app, clear app data through Android, or uninstall Zixia. For information processed by a third-party service, use that provider’s controls and policies to request access, correction, or deletion.

8. Data security

We take reasonable measures to reduce the risk of unauthorized access, disclosure, and misuse. No internet transmission or device environment can be guaranteed completely secure. Protect your device and accounts, and install the APK only from the official website.

9. Children

Children should use Zixia Browser with guidance from a parent or guardian. If you are a guardian and believe a child has provided inappropriate information, contact us using the address below.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this policy when product features, legal requirements, or service practices change. The latest version and effective date will be published on this page, and material changes will be communicated in a reasonable manner.

11. Contact us

For privacy questions, suggestions, or data-rights requests, email icu4ever100@gmail.com.
